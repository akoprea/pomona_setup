PROBLEM: bootctl failed to boot the main partition after update packages with pacman or yay
ERROR: ../systemd/src/boot/boot.c:2649@call_image_start: Error preparing initrd: Not found
PROBABLE-CAUSE: package update was interupted
SOLUTION:
- Boot live usb, `mount` main and boot partitions, `arch-chroot`.
- `pacman -Syu`
- `mkinitcpio -P`
- `bootctl update`
- Exit chroot, unmount everything, and reboot
